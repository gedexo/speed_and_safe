Who We Are

Delivering Quality, Building Global Connections Since 2017

Speed and Safe General Trading is a Dubai-based food import and export company with a strong presence across the Middle East. Since 2017, we have been connecting international food suppliers with regional markets, specializing in the bulk trading of premium meat, poultry, seafood, and a variety of grocery essentials.

Trusted for our quality, transparency, and customer-first approach, we serve clients throughout the UAE and the broader GCC. Our commitment to timely delivery, international standards, and long-term partnerships has made us a preferred name in the global food trade. As we continue to expand our global reach, we remain focused on bridging borders with trust and quality.

Global Food Trade Solutions

Connecting Markets, Delivering Excellence

MISSION

Our mission is to import and export the finest quality food products from around the world, serving the UAE and broader Middle East with integrity and efficiency. We aim to be a trusted partner in global food trade—ensuring consistent supply, competitive pricing, and unmatched customer satisfaction. By bridging global sources with regional demand, we strive to be the preferred choice for reliable food import and export services in the region.

VISION

Our vision is to become a leading name in international food trading based in Dubai—recognized for quality, transparency, and long-term partnerships. We aspire to expand our global reach while maintaining strong roots in the GCC, offering seamless trade solutions and building enduring relationships with suppliers and clients worldwide.

BEST PRICE VALUE

At Speed and Safe General Trading, our import and export team actively monitors global and regional markets to ensure our food products remain competitively priced without ever compromising on quality. We believe affordability should never come at the cost of excellence. By building strong international supplier relationships and leveraging smart sourcing strategies, we deliver high-quality food products at the best possible value for our clients across the UAE and GCC.

QUALITY POLICY

Quality is the foundation of everything we do. At Speed and Safe General Trading, each product in our import-export portfolio is carefully selected through a rigorous quality control process. We verify origins, inspect production practices, and assess packaging integrity before approving any product. From sourcing and international shipping to storage and distribution in the UAE, we uphold strict standards to ensure freshness, safety, and premium quality. Our commitment is to be a trusted name in global food trade, delivering excellence from farm to shelf.

Why Choose Us

Our commitment to quality and safety is unwavering. Every product we import and export undergoes strict quality checks before shipment, with continuous monitoring throughout the entire supply chain to ensure freshness and compliance.
