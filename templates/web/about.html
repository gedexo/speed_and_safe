Who We Are

Delivering Quality, Building Trust Since 2017

Speed and Safe General Trading is a leading and rapidly growing distributor of food and non-food items in the Middle East. As a trusted wholesale supplier, we specialize in bulk grocery trading, serving clients across the UAE and expanding our footprint throughout the GCC.

Established in 2017, we have built a strong reputation for supplying high-quality food products, including meat, poultry, and seafood. Our commitment to excellence, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as one of the most reliable and sought-after wholesale trading companies in the region. With a focus on quality, trust, and long-term relationships, we continue to grow as a preferred partner in food and non-food distribution across the Middle East.

MISSION

Our mission is driven by a strong ambition to deliver the highest quality food and non-food products across the UAE and the wider Middle East. We strive to be a trusted wholesale partner, fully committed to meeting the complete needs of our customers. Our ultimate goal is to become the go-to, single-source supplier for premium food and non-food products throughout the region.

VISION

The vision of Speed and Safe General Trading is to become one of the most sought-after wholesale distributors of food and non-food products in the GCC. We aim to excel in product quality, ensure timely delivery, and foster lasting relationships by making every customer feel valued and respected.

BEST PRICE VALUE

Our marketing team continuously monitors market pricing across the GCC to ensure our products remain competitively priced while maintaining exceptional quality. We understand that compromising on cost often means compromising on quality—and that is never an option for us. At Speed and Safe General Trading, we are committed to offering the best value by securing the most competitive prices without ever lowering our quality standards. Our goal is to provide high-quality food and non-food products that are both reliable and affordable for every customer.

QUALITY POLICY

At Speed and Safe General Trading, we are committed to delivering only the highest quality products. Our quality assurance process is thorough and detailed before any product is added to our portfolio. We carefully evaluate every item—tracing its origin, reviewing production methods, and inspecting final packaging. Our experts are involved in every step, from sourcing to loading for shipment to the UAE. Once in the country, we ensure top-tier storage and maintain exceptional delivery standards. We take great pride in being a trusted supplier of premium food and non-food products across the Middle East.
